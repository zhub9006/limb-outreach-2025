### Key Gaps in Prosthetic Research
1. **Cost-effectiveness data** — Very few trials explicitly measure cost/affordability outcomes
2. **Low-resource settings** — Minimal research on prosthetics for developing countries
3. **3D-printing/rapid manufacturing** — Emerging but under-studied for clinical validation
4. **Long-term abandonment** — Most studies are short-term; few track patients >2 years
5. **Upper limb prosthetics** — Far fewer studies than lower limb
6. **Pediatric prosthetics** — Severely under-represented in clinical research
